Khumai danda Trek

The Khumai Danda trek, also known as the Machhapuchhre Model Trek, is a popular and relatively short trek located near Pokhara, Nepal, offering stunning views of the Annapurna range, including Mt. Machhapuchhre (Fishtail Mountain). It's a great alternative to more crowded routes like Poon Hill. This trek is known for its serene atmosphere, lush forests, and picturesque villages, providing a more off-the-beaten-path experience with opportunities to interact with local Gurung communities.

Difficulty Level: easy
